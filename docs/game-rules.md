# Wallball Rules and Mechanics

This document captures the core wallball-specific rules. Rules that are not
called out here generally follow baseball conventions.

## Field Layout

The field is ordered from the wall outward:

1. Wall
2. Batter
3. Pitcher
4. Infield line
5. Outfield line
6. Fielders
7. Fence

## Pitch Flow

The pitcher throws the pitch toward the batter and the wall.

If the batter swings and misses, or does not swing, the ball reaches the wall.
The pitch result is based on whether the ball hits the wall inside or outside
the square that defines the strike zone.

If the batter hits the ball, the ball travels back toward the pitcher and
fielders. The result is determined by how far the ball travels, where it is
fielded, and whether it is caught before bouncing.

## Hit Results

| Result | Condition |
| --- | --- |
| Fly out | The pitcher or a fielder catches the ball before it bounces. |
| Ground out | The pitcher fields the ball after a bounce before it reaches the infield line. |
| Single | The ball bounces and is fielded between the infield line and the outfield line. |
| Double | The ball bounces and is fielded between the outfield line and the fence. |
| Triple | The ball bounces, hits the fence, and is then fielded. |
| Home run | The ball goes over the fence. |
